About

I am currently a machine learning engineer at XiaoMi Inc. I received my Master degree from the Department of Computer Science, Beijing Jiaotong University, under the supervision of Prof. Jing Wang. My research interests include Anomaly Detection, Self-supervised / Unsupervised Learning and Deep Learning Methods for Physiological Time-series. Recently I am focusing on learning semantic representations for physiological time-series.
